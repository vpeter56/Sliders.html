01 — OVERVIEW

WHO WE ARE

Sliders is a personal day-planning application developed and operated by Vincze Péter ("we", "us", "controller"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights regarding that data.

We are committed to processing your data lawfully, transparently, and only to the extent necessary to provide the service.

02 — DATA COLLECTED

WHAT WE COLLECT

Sliders collects only the minimum data required to provide its features:

EMAIL ADDRESS
Provided via Google Sign-In. Used solely to identify your account. We do not use it for marketing.
TASK DATA
Task names, time allocations, colors, and dates you enter in the app. Stored to enable cross-device sync.
NOT COLLECTED
We do not collect IP addresses, device identifiers, behavioural tracking data, location, or any other personal data beyond the above.
03 — LEGAL BASIS

WHY WE PROCESS IT

Under GDPR Article 6, our legal bases are:

CONTRACT
Processing your email and task data is necessary to provide the core functionality of the service you signed up for (Art. 6(1)(b)).
CONSENT
By creating an account via Google Sign-In, you consent to the processing described in this policy (Art. 6(1)(a)).
04 — DATA STORAGE

WHERE DATA IS STORED

Your data is stored in Supabase, a third-party infrastructure provider. Supabase operates on AWS infrastructure. We configure our Supabase project to use EU-based servers (Frankfurt, eu-central-1) to ensure GDPR compliance.

Supabase implements Row Level Security (RLS), meaning each user can only access their own data — not other users' data, even at the database query level.

Supabase's own Privacy Policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

NOTE ON PAID FEATURES: In the future, Sliders may offer optional paid features. Any payment processing will be handled by a third-party payment provider (e.g. Stripe) and subject to their own privacy policies. This policy will be updated accordingly before any such features are launched.
05 — RETENTION

HOW LONG WE KEEP DATA

ACTIVE ACCOUNT
Data is retained as long as you have an active account.
ACCOUNT DELETION
Upon request or when you delete your account in-app, all your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
INACTIVE ACCOUNTS
Accounts with no login activity for 24 months may be deleted after prior email notice.

07 — COOKIES

COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

Sliders uses browser localStorage to cache your task data locally for offline use. This is not a tracking cookie — it contains only your task data and app preferences, stored only on your own device.

Authentication state is maintained via a Supabase session token stored in localStorage. This is strictly necessary for the login feature to function and does not track behaviour.

We use no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies of any kind.
